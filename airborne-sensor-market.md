<p><ul><li>Non-Scanning</li><li>Scanning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airborne sensor market is classified into two primary types: non-scanning and scanning sensors. Non-scanning sensors collect data from a fixed point, such as cameras or radiometers, providing high-resolution images or measurements over specific areas. In contrast, scanning sensors actively move across a landscape, capturing data by sweeping a range, like LIDAR or synthetic aperture radar. Both types serve various applications, including environmental monitoring, agriculture, and urban planning, catering to distinct operational needs and data requirements.</p></p>
