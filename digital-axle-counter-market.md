<p><strong>The Digital Axle Counter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rail Side Axle Counter</li><li>On-rail Axle Counter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Axle Counter market is segmented into rail side and on-rail axle counters. Rail side axle counters are installed adjacent to the railway track, detecting train axle movements through sensors that relay data to centralized systems for monitoring. On-rail axle counters, on the other hand, are integrated directly onto the trains, providing real-time tracking of axle positions as they traverse the track. Both types enhance safety, improve operational efficiency, and facilitate effective train management in railway systems.</p></p>
