<p><p>The press brakes market comprises four main types: hydraulic, mechanical, pneumatic, and servo motor. Hydraulic press brakes use fluid power for bending, offering high force and precision, making them popular in heavy-duty applications. Mechanical press brakes rely on gears and flywheels for movement, providing speed and efficiency for lighter tasks. Pneumatic press brakes utilize compressed air for quick cycles, ideal for softer materials. Servo motor press brakes combine electric control with high precision, enhancing energy efficiency and adaptability for intricate designs.</p></p>
