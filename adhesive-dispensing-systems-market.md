<p><p>The adhesive dispensing systems market is categorized into manual and automatic systems. In contrast, automatic adhesive dispensing systems utilize machines for precise and consistent adhesive application, ideal for high-volume production environments. These systems increase efficiency, reduce labor costs, and enhance product quality. The choice between manual and automatic systems depends on production needs, scale, and desired precision in adhesive application.</p></p>
