<p><p>The disposable medical nitrile gloves market is categorized into powdered and non-powdered types. Powdered nitrile gloves are coated with a dusting of cornstarch or similar powder, which makes them easier to don and doff, but they may pose an allergy risk for some users. Non-powdered nitrile gloves, on the other hand, do not contain any powder and are less likely to cause allergic reactions, making them a popular choice in clinical settings where sensitivity is a concern.</p></p>
