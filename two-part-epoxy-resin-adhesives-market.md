<p><strong>The Two-Part Epoxy Resin Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid</li><li>Flexible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Two-part epoxy resin adhesives are categorized into rigid and flexible types based on their mechanical properties and applications. Rigid epoxy adhesives cure to form a hard, inflexible bond, offering excellent structural strength and heat resistance, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications like construction and automotive repairs. In contrast, flexible epoxy adhesives maintain elasticity after curing, allowing for movement and stress absorption. This flexibility makes them suitable for applications requiring durability under dynamic loads, such as in electronics and composite bonding.</p></p>
