<p><p>The electric air compressor market is primarily divided into two types: single-stage and two-stage electric air compressors. Single-stage electric air compressors compress air in one cycle and are ideal for lighter tasks, suitable for small jobs and lower pressure applications. In contrast, two-stage electric air compressors complete the compression process in two stages, allowing for higher pressure output and greater efficiency, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types cater to varying user needs based on power and application.</p></p>
