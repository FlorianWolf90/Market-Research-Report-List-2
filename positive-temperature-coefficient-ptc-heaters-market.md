<p><p>Positive Temperature Coefficient (PTC) heaters are electrical heating devices that utilize materials with a positive temperature coefficient to provide efficient heating. This safety feature makes PTC heaters popular in various applications, including home heating, industrial processes, and automotive systems.</p><p>The PTC heaters market is experiencing significant growth due to rising energy efficiency awareness and a growing preference for safe, self-regulating heating solutions.
