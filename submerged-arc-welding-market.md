<p><p>Submerged Arc Welding (SAW) is a highly efficient and automated welding process that involves the formation of an arc between a continuously fed electrode and the workpiece, with the arc being submerged beneath a layer of granular flux. In contrast, automatic submerged arc welding systems are fully automated, enhancing efficiency and precision, particularly for high-volume production.
