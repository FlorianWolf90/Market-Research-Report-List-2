<p><p>The Reactive Emulsifier market comprises primarily of two types: Anionic and Nonionic emulsifiers. Anionic emulsifiers carry a negative charge and are effective in stabilizing emulsions in systems with basic pH, making them suitable for various applications like personal care and pharmaceuticals. Nonionic reactive emulsifiers, on the other hand, are neutral and versatile, providing stability across a wide range of pH and ionic conditions. Both types enhance product properties, improve stability, and play crucial roles in diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
